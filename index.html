Our Story

PointScale is a student-led web design agency focused on creating clean, modern websites for local businesses. We believe that great web design shouldn't be complicated or expensive. Our team combines fresh perspectives with technical expertise to deliver websites that not only look great but also drive results.

We specialize in minimal, user-friendly designs that help businesses establish a strong online presence and connect with their customers effectively. As students, we bring innovation, creativity, and the latest design trends to every project.
